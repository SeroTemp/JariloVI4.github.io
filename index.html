What to do in JARILO-VI

Traversing across the snow plains, you can get a full view of Belobog, the only spot on the ice-cold world of Jarilo-VI that barely fits the definition of "a warm place." As the only remaining city with human civilization, Belobog has been given many grandiose titles: the last bastion of mankind against the Eternal Freeze, the land blessed by Qlipoth.
